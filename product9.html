1/12th Collectible Action Figure

VTOYS X BMS X CF Cyberpunk Robin series is under the Cyberpunk product line, which is well-known for its highly-poseable 1/12th scale figures, detailed sculpt, and tailor-made outfits to capture the memorable appearance as seen in the original series.

Cyberpunk Robin is approximately 6 inches (18.5cm) tall, and is a fully-articulated collectible figure with over 27 points of articulation. The figure is sculpted by renowned sculptor V, and the materials used for the figure are mainly PVC and POM.
