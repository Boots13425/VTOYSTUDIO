1/12th Collectible Action Figure

USD $70 / RMB ¥398

ROMANKYE X VTOYS 1/12 MUSE series is under the Asura product line, which is well-known for its highly-poseable 1/12th scale figures, detailed sculpt, and tailor-made outfits to capture the memorable appearance as seen in the original series.

ROMANKYE X VTOYS 1/12 MUSE is approximately 6 inches (15.8cm) tall, and is a fully-articulated collectible figure with over 27 points of articulation. The figure was painted by renowned painter SoyooNG（Korea） and carved by sculptor V, and the materials used for the figure are mainly PVC and POM.
