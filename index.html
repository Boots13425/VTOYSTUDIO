BRAND INTRODUCTION

Vtoys Studio，founded in Shanghai in 2019； Focus on the design，production and sale of derivative. The aim is to create China's high-end derivatives brand. The studio is based on 3D modeling and 3D printing as the main development mode, which brings together many outstanding artists and designers abroad to participate in the design and development work.
